African bush elephant

Loxodonta africana

The African bush elephant (Loxodonta africana) is the larger of the two species of African elephant. Both it and the African forest elephant have usually been classified as a single species, known simply as the African elephant, but recent preliminary evidence has seen the forest elephant classified as a distinct species.

Where can I learn more?

You can learn more about African bush elephants by visiting the African Wildlife Foundation website.
